LNG & LPG

LNG & LPG Infrastructure and Logistics

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has approved AmerigroupLNG to export approximately 4.6 billion cubic feet (Bcf) of liquefied natural gas (LNG) from the United States. This significant achievement underscores our commitment to providing high-quality energy solutions and expanding our global reach.

Amerigroup is a trusted partner in clean fuel mobility, energy transition logistics, and regasification infrastructure across the Americas and beyond. Our long-standing presence in U.S. – Mexico LNG and LPG corridors provides the foundation for scalable, reliable energy solutions:

LNG and LPG Transportation: End-to-end transport solutions by ship, truck, ISO container, and pipeline, ensuring secure delivery across borders

Onshore Regasification: Localized conversion of LNG back to natural gas for utilities, factories, and power grids

FSRU (Floating Storage and Regasification Units): Rapid-deployment offshore terminals that provide LNG import and regas capacity without the need for land-based infrastructure. Ideal for port cities, island nations, and countries facing land constraints or urgent energy needs

Marine Bunkering: Supplying LNG as a cleaner fuel for maritime fleets

Comprehensive LNG Chain Management: From export and storage to regasification and dispatch, we offer full-scope LNG handling — pump, load, move, vaporize, and deliver, all with efficiency and safety at scale

Small-Scale Distribution: Delivering LNG and LPG to off-grid, rural, or remote industrial sites

Renewable Energy

Solar and Wind Power Generation

Amerigroup is at the forefront of integrating renewable energy sources into our clean energy solutions. Our solar and wind power generation projects are designed to harness the abundant natural resources available, converting them into sustainable electricity. This electricity is then used to produce hydrogen and ammonia, creating a continuous cycle of clean energy production. Our services include:

  • Solar Power Plants: Developing and managing large-scale solar power facilities that convert sunlight into electricity.
  • Wind Farms: Establishing and operating wind farms that capture wind energy and convert it into electrical power.
  • Electrolysis for Hydrogen Production: Using renewable electricity to split water into hydrogen and oxygen, providing a sustainable source of hydrogen.
  • Ammonia Production: Utilizing hydrogen produced from renewable sources to create ammonia, a key component for energy storage and agricultural applications.

E-Mobility

EV Conversion

We specialize in the conversion of Class 5 and 8 trucks from diesel to electric using cutting-edge e-axle technology. Our EV conversion services help reduce diesel consumption and pollution while significantly increasing travel range, up to 1,300 miles. Our notable clients include: Staples, UPS, FedEx, Kenworth etc.

Long-Term Operational Benefits: Evaluating the financial and environmental benefits of hydrogen trucks and EV conversions, including cost savings, reduced maintenance expenses, and improved sustainability.
