Mr. Gerardo Payán Ramos

President, Amerigroup International | President & Founder, AmerigroupLNG | Founder of AmeriGas Propane S.A. de C.V.

Mr. Gerardo Payán Ramos serves as President in Amerigroup International, Founder and CEO of AmerigroupLNG, as well as the founder of AmeriGas Propane S.A. de C.V. in Mexico. With over 15 years in the energy industry, he is steering these companies toward becoming leading global providers of integrated energy solutions. Specializing in government relations and strategic partnerships between Mexico and the USA, Gerardo has become an authority in emerging energy fields — ranging from LNG and hydrogen to solar, wind, and EV conversion.

Mr. Ken Wong

Corporate Vice President

Mr. Ken Wong, a Canadian-Chinese entrepreneur, serves as Corporate Vice President and Co-Founder of Amerigroup International, where he directs the company's global strategy and operations. His entrepreneurial journey spans commodity trading and mining across Canada, the USA, and Mexico. Notably, in 2012, he became the first Canadian-Chinese exporter of iron ore from Mexico to China — a milestone recognized by SGS. Since 2016, he has played a significant role in Belt and Road projects across Africa, contributing to initiatives in Gabon, Guinea, Cameroon, South Africa, and Morocco.

Mr. James Wang

CEO (China)

Mr. James Wang, CEO for China at Amerigroup International, steers the company's strategic vision and operations in the Chinese market. With over 15 years of experience at Fortune 500 companies and leading Chinese corporations, he excels in managing government relations and partnering with state-owned enterprises. He has overseen transactions exceeding $10 billion and played a key role in Belt and Road projects across several African countries.

Mr. Roberto Compeán Woodworth

CFO (Mexico), Amerigroup International | CFO & Co-founder, AmerigroupLNG

Mr. Roberto Compeán Woodworth, CFO of Amerigroup International for the Mexico region and co-founder of AmerigroupLNG, drives the company's financial strategy. His leadership includes Chief Sales Officer at Mexico's National Lottery and CFO for Bestel and Cablevision Monterrey — key Televisa subsidiaries. His tenure at BBVA Bank, including advanced training in Madrid, solidified his expertise in finance.

Mr. Peter Pang

CFO (CGA/CFA) Global

Mr. Peter Pang, CFO of Amerigroup International, is a distinguished financial leader with over 30 years of industry experience. As a former partner at KPMG Vancouver and a Chartered Professional Accountant in Canada, he brings deep expertise in auditing, financial management, and mergers and acquisitions. His strategic leadership in managing multi-billion-dollar enterprises has earned him a global reputation.

Mr. Victor Manuel Villanueva Rivera

Managing Director

Mr. Victor Manuel Villanueva Rivera is a seasoned executive with over 40 years in the automotive industry. Previous roles include Managing Director at Jetour Motors and FIAT Mexico, Sales Director at Hertz-AVASA, and CEO & Managing Director of Mitsubishi Motors Mexico.

Ms. Vanessa Muñoz

Business Development Director

Ms. Vanessa Muñoz acts a key role in developing new business opportunities. She has successfully owned and operated various businesses in Mexico and the U.S. Previously Real Estate Commercial and Leasing Manager for Liverpool, and at Alsea facilitated strategic negotiations with major developers.

Ms. Liviere Galván

Commercial Director

Ms. Liviere Galván transitioned from Sales Manager to Commercial Director, driving the company's sales strategy, new business development, and international client expansion. Prior to Amerigroup, she served as Director of Sales and Marketing at Dronamex for over five years in Mexico.
