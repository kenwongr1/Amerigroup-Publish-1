Our History

From Regional LNG Transporter to Global Green Energy Leader

Amerigroup has grown from a regional LNG transporter to a global leader in green energy solutions. Over the past few decades, we have expanded our expertise to include hydrogen production, power grid modernization projects, and advanced clean energy infrastructure, consistently pushing the boundaries of innovation and sustainability. This legacy of growth fuels our commitment to meet today's climate and energy challenges with proven, integrated, and forward-thinking solutions.

Amerigroup envisions a future where clean energy systems are seamlessly integrated across sectors, enabling sustainable industrial growth, resilient communities, and zero-emission transportation networks. Through relentless innovation, robust partnerships, and uncompromising operational excellence, we empower clients and governments to lead the global climate agenda.
