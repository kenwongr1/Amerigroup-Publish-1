Renewable Energy Generation: Solar, Wind Turbines & Water Solar Solutions

Amerigroup integrates cutting-edge solar photovoltaic systems, utility-scale wind turbines, and innovative water solar solutions such as floating solar arrays to maximize clean energy production. Hydrogen Production & Dispensing Systems

Harnessing cutting-edge electrolysis and blue hydrogen technologies with carbon capture, Amerigroup designs and deploys modular, scalable hydrogen production facilities. Our hydrogen refueling stations are engineered for industrial, transportation, and power generation applications — empowering the shift towards a zero-carbon hydrogen economy.

Methanol Fuel Cell Power Generation & Range Extenders

We deliver industry-leading methanol fuel cell generators and range extender systems, providing clean, reliable power for off-grid locations, emergency backup, and electric mobility. Our innovative methanol solutions combine efficiency, portability, and environmental performance, enabling seamless energy access in diverse operational contexts.

Advanced Linear Generator Technology

At the forefront of sustainable energy generation, this technology converts mechanical energy directly into electrical power with minimal moving parts. By employing a linear motion mechanism rather than traditional rotating components, this generator offers enhanced efficiency, longer operational lifespans, and greater reliability. Its compact design and scalability make it suitable for a wide range of applications, from distributed energy systems to grid support and emergency power supply, ensuring resilient and consistent energy output.

Hydrogen Injection Reactor

A cutting-edge system designed to enhance fuel efficiency and reduce emissions in transport units by using only water as the source for hydrogen production. The reactor utilizes electrolysis to generate hydrogen on-demand, which is directly injected into the engine's combustion chamber. This seamless integration with existing engines results in optimized performance, cleaner fuel combustion, and reduced reliance on fossil fuels. The system is engineered for durability, low maintenance, and adaptability across different vehicle platforms.

LNG Onshore Regasification & Infrastructure

Amerigroup operates state-of-the-art onshore LNG regasification terminals designed for optimal throughput, safety, and integration with downstream gas networks. Our infrastructure services encompass LNG storage, vaporization, pipeline injection, and comprehensive logistics, facilitating secure and flexible natural gas supply for utilities and industries.

Offshore Floating Storage and Regasification Units (FSRU)

Our advanced offshore FSRU solutions offer agile, cost-effective alternatives to traditional land-based terminals. Strategically deployed in ports and coastal hubs worldwide, Amerigroup's FSRUs accelerate LNG import capacity with minimal environmental footprint and rapid commissioning timelines, addressing urgent energy demands with unmatched reliability.

Overseas LNG Supply Chain Management

Amerigroup ensures end-to-end LNG supply chain excellence from international procurement, marine transportation, and cargo handling to distribution and delivery underpinned by rigorous safety standards and real-time monitoring systems.
